About us

Big ideas can start small.

Tee Squared is a local screen printing and custom apparel shop in Northeast Minneapolis. We make high-quality printing and design services accessible to individuals, businesses, groups, and organizations in a laid-back creative environment. Every order gets the same care and attention to detail.
